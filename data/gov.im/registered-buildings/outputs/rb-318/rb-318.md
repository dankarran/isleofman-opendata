Register entry description 
 
History 
 
The memorial was unveiled on 08 November 1923 by the Lieutenant Governor Sir William Fry 
KCVO CB. It was dedicated by the Lord Bishop of Sodor and Man.  
 
It was designed by P.M.C. Kermode and constructed by T.H. Royston. 
 
Details 
 
FORM and MATERIALS: Celtic cross resting on an inclined base which sits on five tiered steps.
