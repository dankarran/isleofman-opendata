Register entry description 
 
History 
 
The memorial is dedicated to those on the island that fell in the First World War (referred to as 
the Great War 1914-1918 on the inscription). The exact date of the memorial is unknown, 
thought to be 1920s. 
 
Details 
 
FORM and MATERIALS: 2 sandstone plaques set into and next to each other within a walled 
semi-circle behind a sandstone 3 tiered octagonal column. Sandstone cross in centre. Bronze 
sword on front face of cross.
