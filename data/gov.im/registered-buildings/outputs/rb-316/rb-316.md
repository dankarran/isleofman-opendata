Register entry description 
 
History 
 
The memorial was unveiled on 11 November 1921 and dedicated by Rev. H.S. Taggart, M.A., 
Vicar of St. Matthew's Church, Douglas. 
 
The monument was a gift from Mr John Leigh Goldie-Taubman of the Nunnery, Douglas. 
 
The 1935-1945 inscription was added later using a separate tablet. 
 
Details 
 
FORM and MATERIALS: Irish limestone cross, located within enclosed iron railings on Douglas 
Head, facing towards Douglas Bay, above Marine Drive. The 1935-45 tablet is made from 
Manx slate.
