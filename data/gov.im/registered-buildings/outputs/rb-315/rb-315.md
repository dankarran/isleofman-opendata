Register entry description 
 
History 
The memorial was unveiled by Lord Derby on 29th May 1924. 
 
The memorial includes names from the First World War, Second World War and Aden (1966). 
 
The architect was Ewart Crellin, Douglas. The sculptor was Harry Hemms & Sons, of Exeter 
and Thomas Quayle, Douglas. 
 
Details 
FORM and MATERIALS: Statue of a service man in full kit standing on top of column. 
 
On the west face there is a laurel wreath above main inscription and a gold cross near the top 
of the column.  
 
On the east face there is a cross in stonework near top of column, corresponding with the gold 
cross on west face.
