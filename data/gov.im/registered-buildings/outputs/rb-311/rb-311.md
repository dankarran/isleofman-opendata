Register entry description 
 
History 
 
The memorial was unveiled on 6th April 1921 by Lieutenant Governor Major-General Sir 
William Fry, K.C.V.O., C.B. 
 
Details 
 
FORM and MATERIALS: Memorial set in a yellow sandstone plinth with a Celtic cross at the 
top. There are two bronze plaques towards the base. 
 
 
Reason for Registration Decision 
 
Ballaugh Parish War Memorial is recommended for entry into the Protected Buildings Register 
for the following reasons: 
● HISTORIC INTEREST: 
• 
The memorial commemorates members of the community that were lost in conflict.
