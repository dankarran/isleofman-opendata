Register entry description 
 
History 
 
Unveiled on Friday 28 July 1922 by Sir George Beatson, M.D, K.C.B. 
 
Dedicated by Right Rev. James Denton Thompson, Bishop of Man. 
 
Designed by Ronald F. Dodd of Oxford, son of T.M. Dodd of Castletown. 
Built by messrs W.H. Avtell and co. of Oxford. 
 
Details 
 
FORM and MATERIALS: Constructed from Cornish grey granite with Celtic cross on top and 
sword with Manx emblem on main column. Names are on bronze tablets.
